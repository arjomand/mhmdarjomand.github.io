Mohammad Arjomand

Email: arjomand.m (at) gmail (dot) com

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Engineer II in the School of Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech), where I am working with Prof. Moinuddin K Qureshi.

My research interests focus on two related topics. The first is computer architecture including design and optimization of microprocesssors, interconnection network, memory systems and storage with focus on using emerging technologies. The second is computer systems including design and optimizations at operating system and compilers.

I recieved my PhD (2014) and MSc (2008) in Computer Architecture (Hardware Engineerin) from Department of Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology, Iran. During my PhD, I worked on "Design of Memory Systems with Non-Volatile Technologies" under supervision of Prof. Hamid Sarbazi-Azad.
